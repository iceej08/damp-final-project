Vegetarian Chickpea Sandwich Filling

visibility 1,302
favorite 584
bookmark 56
JK
Janice Kusuma
access_time 20 minutes
restaurant 1 serving
Tags: Lunch Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1 can chick pea, drained and rinsed
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

Step 2

Mash chickpeas in a medium bowl with a fork.

Step 3

Mix in celery, onion, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and dill until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Serve on bread and enjoy!
